 The english language as we know it is a Germanic language specifically within the West Germanic language family, along with other major languages such as German and Dutch. The Germanic languages all originated in a proto language we now refer to as Proto-Germanic. This proto language has several key features, like sounds and sound changes over time, that distinguish it from its parent language; another proto language referred to today as Proto-Indo-European.
